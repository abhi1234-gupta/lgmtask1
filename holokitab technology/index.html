about US

We are an early-stage EdTech startup based in Punjab. Our mission is to make learning more interactive and engaging by using textbooks. We believe that textbooks are the best way to understand and retain information for a more extended period than videos that are a passive way of learning. So, we are using Augmented Reality to make textbooks more interesting for students.
